Brazil | Caipirinha

The cocktail was originally belived to have been used as a medicine to ease the affects of the Spanish flu. The original recipe consisted of Cachaça, green lemon, honey and garlic. Green lemon was used for its high concentration of vitamin C and Cachaça contained alcohol needed to expedite the absorbtion of the vitamins in the organism. Since then the honey and garlic have been substituted with ice and sugar creating the popular cocktail.

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Garnish: Lime wedge
  • Glass: Old Fashioned Glass

Ingredients:

  • 1 Lime wedge
  • 3tsp Golden caster sugar
  • 100ml Cachaça
  • Ice

Method:

Put the lime wedge and sugar into a mug and use the end of a rolling pin to break them down. Release as much juice from the limes as possible and mix with the sugar to create a syrup. Discard the peel of the lime. Add ice into your chosen glass and pour the syrup mix over, then add Cachaça and garnish with lime wedge.
